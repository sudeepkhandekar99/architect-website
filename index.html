Arcana Architects and Interiors is a One Stop Solution for all your Architectural and Interior designing needs. Our Services
01

Consultation

It’s challenging to decide if you require to make changes in your house and the procedure for the same. We help you with understanding the functions according to the spaces and suggest you with the suitable and best designs that add luxury and comfort.

02

Concept, Designing and Space Planning

Selecting an appropriate design is difficult when you have multiple options. We develop the concept for the site and provide you with the accurate layouts & designs considering the Vaastu. Followed by, detailed drawings suitable for site execution.

03

Renovation

Renovating your house is a tedious process. May it be simple changes right from repainting to changing the design of your interiors, we are here for you.

04

Furniture Designing

Interiors are incomplete without the right designs of your Furniture. We make customized furniture as per the size of your house. From Dining Tables to Wardrobe Cabinets, we provide you with end to end professional assistance.

05

Civil Work

Well, its tedious to deal with plumbers and carpenters individually! We solve your problem by providing you with the right person and resolve your issue without your assistance.

06

Art Fabrics and Material Selections

Finding it difficult to decide the best materials and fabrics? We help you choose the perfect materials and art fabrics that is pocket friendly.
